About Me

Hi, I'm Bruna.

I'm a passionate front-end developer with a knack for creating visually stunning and user-friendly web applications. With a strong foundation in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, I specialize in crafting responsive designs that adapt seamlessly across devices. My journey in web development is fueled by a love for innovation and a commitment to delivering exceptional digital experiences.

Still in training, but already immersed in the world of digital creation. I'm exploring tools and concepts to transform ideas into real and functional projects. In this portfolio, I share some of my work and learnings so far - and this is just the beginning.

Formation

My educational background includes a strong foundation in computer science and web development principles. I've completed courses in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and responsive design, with a focus on creating modern and accessible web applications.

Experience

I've worked on various projects that have helped me refine my skills in front-end development. My experience includes collaborating with teams to build user-friendly interfaces and implementing responsive designs that work across all devices.

Tools

I'm proficient in using tools such as Visual Studio Code, Git, and various frameworks like React. I also have experience with design tools like Figma for creating wireframes and prototypes.
